## Weekly Schedule Template

### Monday — Authority Day

**TikTok (post 1, 13:00 UTC):** Weekly AI news reaction
- Hook: "OpenAI / Anthropic / [major AI company] just changed everything. Here's what nobody is telling you."
- Length: 45–60s
- Format: Fast-cut commentary over B-roll or screen recording
- Goal: catches Monday news scroll audience

**TikTok (post 2, 19:00 UTC):** AI income proof
- Hook: income-proof or bold_claim style
- Length: 60–90s
- Format: income report or case study walk-through
- Goal: motivational, high save rate

**TikTok (post 3, 00:00 UTC):** AI tool list video
- Hook: "5 free AI tools that [replace expensive subscription]"
- Length: 60–90s
- Format: numbered list, one tool per segment
- Goal: late-night discovery audience, saves

**Instagram (15:00 UTC):** Weekly AI tools carousel
- Slide 1: "The [X] AI Tools Worth Knowing This Week"
- Slides 2–9: one tool per slide (name, what it does, cost saved)
- Slide 10: "Save this — follow for weekly drops"
- Goal: saves → Explore placement for the week

**Instagram Stories (16:00, 19:00, 22:00 UTC):** 3 stories
- Story 1: teaser of the carousel ("dropped a big one today ↓")
- Story 2: poll ("which AI tool are you using?")
- Story 3: affiliate CTA for that week's featured tool

**Twitter/X thread (13:00 UTC):** Weekly AI market commentary thread
- 6–8 tweets covering: major AI news, what it means for income, one tool recommendation
- Include affiliate link at tweet 7 with disclosure
- Reply to 3–5 major AI account tweets (reply farming) in the 8–10 AM window

## Content Variation Rules

### Hooks Rotation
Never repeat the same hook type twice in a row within any platform. Rotate between:
1. bold_claim
2. curiosity_gap
3. personal_story
4. counter_intuitive
5. problem_solution

### Affiliate Rotation Schedule
Rotate featured affiliates to avoid audience fatigue:
- Week 1: Jasper AI or Copy.ai (writing tools)
- Week 2: Beehiiv or ConvertKit (newsletter/email)
- Week 3: Systeme.io or Hostinger (business setup)
- Week 4: Synthesia or Murf AI (video/voice AI tools)

### Cross-Platform Adaptation Rules
When repurposing content across platforms:
- TikTok → Instagram Reel: Remove TikTok watermark, add IG hashtags, adjust caption length
- Twitter thread → TikTok: Convert to script, add voiceover, keep same info but visual format
- Instagram carousel → Twitter: Convert slide text to thread tweets
- Never post identical content across platforms — always adapt

**Twitter fallback:** Ask a poll question to the audience
