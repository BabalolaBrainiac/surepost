## FTC Compliance Requirements

All affiliate content MUST include disclosure. The CONTENT_CREATION_AGENT will auto-append these.

**TikTok caption disclosure (required by law):**
"This video contains affiliate links. I may earn a commission if you sign up using my link at no extra cost to you. #ad"

**Instagram caption disclosure:**
"#ad — affiliate link in bio. I earn a commission on sign-ups."

**Twitter/X disclosure:**
"Affiliate link below — I earn a commission on sign-ups." (or use #ad hashtag)

**Important:** Disclosures must appear in the first 3 lines of any caption. Not hidden in "more" or at the end.

## Revenue Modeling Per Affiliate

### Conservative Monthly Projections by Channel Size

**At 10,000 followers (Month 2–3):**
- Profile link CTR: ~2% of engaged audience per week = ~200 clicks/month
- Affiliate conversion rate: 5% average = 10 sign-ups/month
- Average commission: $20 blended = $200/month affiliate only

**At 50,000 followers (Month 4–5):**
- Profile link CTR: ~2.5% = 1,250 clicks/month
- Conversion rate: 6% = 75 sign-ups/month
- Average commission: $22 = $1,650/month

**At 100,000 followers (Month 6–8):**
- Profile link CTR: ~3% = 3,000 clicks/month
- Conversion rate: 7% = 210 sign-ups/month
- Average commission: $25 = $5,250/month

**At 280,000 followers (Month 12):**
- Profile link CTR: ~3.5% = 9,800 clicks/month
- Conversion rate: 8% = 784 sign-ups/month
- Average commission: $28 blended (recurring compounding) = $21,952/month affiliate alone

**Key compound effect:** Recurring commissions from months 1–11 continue earning in month 12 and beyond. By month 12, you have 11 months of recurring commissions stacking on top of each other.

## Tracking and Attribution

Each affiliate link should be tracked with UTM parameters and platform-specific sub-IDs:

```
Base affiliate link: ?via=aiincomelab
TikTok sub-ID: ?via=aiincomelab&sub1=tiktok
Instagram sub-ID: ?via=aiincomelab&sub1=instagram
Twitter sub-ID: ?via=aiincomelab&sub1=twitter
```

This allows the ANALYTICS_AGENT to see which platform drives the most affiliate conversions and adjust content strategy accordingly.
