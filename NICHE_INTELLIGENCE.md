### TikTok Algorithm Signals (2025 Weighting)

Priority 2: **Saves and shares** — TikTok 2025 algorithm weights saves and shares as "strong watch intent signals"
Priority 3: **Comments** — especially question-type comments that drive reply threads
Priority 4: **Rewatches** — algorithm interprets rewatch as high-value content
Priority 5: **Follows from video** — measures conversion efficiency

**Key insight:** Carousels outperform single images by 114% in likes/comments/shares AND have the highest save rate. Saves are the key metric for Explore placement. One highly saved carousel resurfaces in Explore for 30–60 days post-publish.

**Reel vs Carousel trade-off:** Use Reels for reach (algorithm distributes widely to non-followers), use carousels for depth (saves, follows, affiliate conversions). Both daily.

### Instagram Carousel Blueprint (Maximum Save Rate)

10-slide formula validated across finance/AI niche accounts:

- Slide 1: Hook statement (full screen, large text). Must answer: "why should I save this?"
- Slide 2: Intro context (what problem this solves)
- Slides 3–8: Core content (one item per slide, consistent visual template)
- Slide 9: Summary / key takeaway
- Slide 10: CTA ("Save this — you'll need it. Follow for weekly drops.")

Typography: Bold, minimal text. Max 3 lines per slide. High contrast backgrounds.

## Competitive Landscape Analysis

### Top Performing Account Archetypes in AI × Money Niche

These are the account types dominating engagement and monetization (not specific accounts, but the patterns):

**Archetype 1: "The Tester"**
Tests AI tools, reports results honestly. "I tested X so you don't have to."
Why it works: removes the risk/effort barrier for audience, builds trust through results.
Monetization fit: very high — audience converts on tool recommendations.

**Archetype 2: "The Income Reporter"**
Documents income journeys. Monthly income reports. Building in public.
Why it works: social proof + narrative arc = compulsive following.
Monetization fit: very high — affiliate + brand deals.

**Archetype 3: "The System Builder"**
Shows how to automate income using AI systems. Technical but accessible.
Why it works: audience aspires to the outcome, follows for the playbook.
Monetization fit: high — digital products, courses, consulting conversions.

**Archetype 4: "The Deal Hunter"**
Finds free/cheap alternatives to expensive tools. "Replace [$X/month] with this free AI."
Why it works: immediate perceived value, massive save rate (they save it to use later).
Monetization fit: high — affiliate for the alternatives being recommended.

**Our positioning:** Blend of Archetype 1 (tester credibility) + Archetype 4 (cost-saving value) with regular income reporting (Archetype 2). This maximizes trust, save rate, and affiliate conversion simultaneously.
