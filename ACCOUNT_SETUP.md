**Why AI × Money wins every comparison:**

**Sub-niche positioning: "I test AI tools so you don't have to — and I show you exactly which ones earn money."**

This is different from generic "AI tools" content. The income angle attracts:
1. People trying to make money online (huge audience)

3. Creators looking for affiliate tools to promote

## Platform Strategy

### TikTok — Primary Growth Engine

**Why TikTok is the priority:**
TikTok's For You Page (FYP) algorithm distributes content based on engagement signals, not follower count. A brand new account can hit 1 million views on its first video. This is the fastest path to the 10,000 followers needed to unlock Creator Rewards.

**Monetization unlocks:**
- 10,000 followers + 100,000 views in 30 days → Creator Rewards ($0.40–$1.00 RPM)
- 50,000 followers → Digital product launch
- 100,000+ followers → Brand deals ($1,000–$5,000 per video)

**Content RPM on TikTok by format:**
- Finance/AI tools tutorials: $1.50–$3.00 RPM
- Generic lifestyle: $0.05–$0.50 RPM
- That is a 30–60x difference for the same number of views

**Posting volume: 3 videos per day**

---

### Instagram — Brand Deal and Save Machine

**Why Instagram is important:**
Instagram carousels get saved and resurface in Explore for weeks. A single viral carousel can drive consistent traffic for 30–60 days. Brand deals on Instagram pay more than TikTok per post once you reach 100K+. Instagram Subscriptions activate at 1K followers ($0.99–$9.99/month per subscriber).

**Monetization unlocks:**
- 1,000 followers → Instagram Subscriptions
- 10,000 followers → Affiliate link directly in Stories
- 50,000+ followers → Brand deals ($500–$3,000 per post)
- High save rate → Consistent Explore page placement → compound organic growth

**Posting: 1 Reel + 1 Carousel per day**

---

### Twitter/X — Revenue Share and Community

**Why Twitter is the long game:**
X Premium revenue share pays on verified-user impressions. Finance/AI content gets heavy engagement from premium users who are tech-savvy professionals. Threads with real alpha (actionable AI income insights) get bookmarked and quoted by larger accounts, compounding reach for free.

**X Revenue Share eligibility:** 500 followers + X Premium subscription + maintain 5M impressions over 3 months. Estimated $0.008–$0.012 per 1,000 verified-user impressions.

**Posting: 2 threads + 5 standalone tweets per day. Reply to @OpenAI, @sama, @AnthropicAI, @levelsio for reply farming on high-traffic posts.**
